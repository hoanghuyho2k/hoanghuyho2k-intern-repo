
# Company Reflection – Focus Bear

## Why do you think Focus Bear was created?

From what I learned on the Focus Bear website, the app was created because Jeremy and many of the team members personally struggled with productivity and focus due to ADHD or autism. They tried many productivity tools but felt that most of them were designed for neurotypical people and did not work well for how their brains function.

Because of this, they decided to build a tool that would actually help people like them stay focused, follow routines, and reduce distractions. Instead of just reminding users to be productive, the app actively guides routines and blocks distractions so users can stay on track.

## What problem is Focus Bear solving?

Focus Bear is trying to solve problems related to **distraction, lack of structure, and difficulty starting tasks**. Many people struggle to stay focused because there are constant distractions from phones, social media, and notifications.

This problem can be even more challenging for people with ADHD or autism because they may struggle with executive functioning, which includes starting tasks, managing time, and maintaining routines.

Focus Bear helps address this by blocking distracting apps and websites, guiding users through routines like morning habits, and encouraging breaks so people do not burn out.

## Why do you think this mission is important?

I think this mission is important because productivity challenges affect a lot of people, especially students and people working in technology or knowledge-based jobs.

Many people are capable and intelligent but struggle to stay organized or focused because of constant digital distractions. Tools that support better routines and healthier work habits can help people perform better in school, work, and everyday life.

For people with ADHD or similar challenges, having a tool that understands how their brain works can make a big difference in their confidence and ability to complete tasks.

## How does Focus Bear’s work align with your personal values or interests?

As someone studying software development and working toward becoming a professional developer, I find it meaningful to work on software that helps people improve their daily lives.

Technology is often associated with distractions, but Focus Bear is trying to use technology in a positive way by helping people build better habits and stay focused. I think that is an interesting and meaningful goal.

I am also interested in building tools that solve real problems, not just theoretical ones, so being involved in a product that helps people manage focus and productivity aligns with my interests.

## Do you personally relate to any of the challenges that Focus Bear aims to solve?

Yes, I can relate to some of the challenges Focus Bear is trying to solve. As a student, it can sometimes be difficult to stay focused for long periods of time, especially when working on a computer where there are many distractions like social media or other websites.

I also know friends and classmates who struggle with procrastination, staying organized, or maintaining consistent routines while studying at university.

An app like Focus Bear could help by creating more structure during study time, blocking distractions, and encouraging healthier work habits.
