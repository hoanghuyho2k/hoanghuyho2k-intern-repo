
# Professionalism at Focus Bear – Reflection

## Professional vs Unprofessional Behaviour

Professional behaviour in a casual work environment includes:
- communicating clearly and respectfully
- being responsible for tasks and deadlines
- listening to others and collaborating effectively
- being open to feedback and willing to improve

Unprofessional behaviour includes:
- ignoring messages or deadlines
- using rude or unclear communication
- blaming others instead of taking responsibility
- showing disrespect or negative attitudes toward teammates

## Respectful Communication in Remote Teams

Respectful communication means:
- using a clear and polite tone in messages
- responding in a timely manner
- asking questions when unsure instead of assuming
- handling disagreements calmly and professionally
- respecting different perspectives and backgrounds

## Giving and Receiving Feedback

When giving feedback:
- focus on actions, not the person
- be constructive and specific
- suggest improvements clearly

When receiving feedback:
- listen without becoming defensive
- ask for clarification if needed
- use feedback as an opportunity to improve

## Reflection

### Experience with teamwork

I have experienced effective teamwork where communication was clear and team members supported each other. What made it effective was mutual respect, open communication, and everyone taking responsibility for their tasks.

### Improving my communication

To ensure my communication is clear and respectful, I will:
- write messages carefully and professionally
- avoid unclear or rushed responses
- confirm understanding when needed

### Creating a positive environment

I can contribute to a positive environment by:
- being friendly and respectful
- supporting teammates when needed
- maintaining a positive attitude even when challenges arise

### Preparing for meetings

To prepare for meetings, I will:
- review the agenda in advance
- prepare questions or updates
- be punctual and attentive

### Being proactive

Being proactive means:
- asking questions when I am unsure
- taking initiative to solve problems
- keeping teammates updated on my progress
- seeking feedback regularly

### Following up and escalation

If I need information or action from someone, I will:
- send a polite follow-up message
- clearly explain what is needed and by when

If there is no response:
- follow up again after a reasonable time
- escalate to a supervisor if the issue is blocking progress or urgent

### Things I will avoid

- ignoring responsibilities or deadlines
- communicating in a disrespectful or unclear way
- blaming others instead of taking responsibility
- sharing inappropriate or sensitive information
- engaging in negative or toxic behaviour

## Key Learning

Professionalism is not about being formal at all times, but about being respectful, responsible, and collaborative. Maintaining a positive and supportive attitude helps create a productive and enjoyable work environment.
