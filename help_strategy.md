
# Help Strategy Reflection

## When do you prefer using AI vs. searching Google?

I prefer using AI tools like ChatGPT when I need explanations, examples, or guidance on how to approach a problem. AI is especially useful for understanding concepts, debugging logic, or generating code snippets.

I use Google when I need quick, factual answers such as specific error messages, documentation, or known issues. Google is helpful for finding official resources and community discussions.

## How do you decide when to ask a colleague instead?

I ask a colleague when the issue is specific to the project, involves business logic, or when I have already tried solving it using AI and Google without success. I also ask for help when the problem is urgent and blocking progress.

## What challenges do developers face when troubleshooting alone?

Developers often face challenges such as spending too much time stuck on a problem, misunderstanding error messages, or going in the wrong direction. Without guidance, it is easy to overlook simple solutions or become inefficient. Collaboration helps reduce these challenges and improves problem-solving efficiency.
